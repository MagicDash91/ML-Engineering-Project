## Marketing Strategy: Retaining At-Risk Banking Customers (BBCA & BMRI)

### 1. Executive Summary

This strategy outlines a comprehensive marketing approach for BBCA and BMRI to address and reduce customer churn among at-risk segments in Indonesia. By leveraging each brand's unique strengths, we will deliver targeted value propositions, messaging, and experiences across digital-first channels. The strategy aims to achieve significant reductions in overall churn, improve contract conversions, enhance new customer retention, increase digital tech support adoption, and mitigate churn among high-charge customers within 6-9 months.

### 2. Overall Strategy Vision

To transform at-risk banking customers into loyal advocates by providing tailored, value-driven, and digitally-empowered financial experiences that address their specific needs for transparency, efficiency, security, and personalized support. We will position BBCA as the trusted leader for secure and seamless digital banking, and BMRI as the innovative hub for comprehensive, connected financial solutions.

### 3. Brand Positioning

#### BBCA Positioning: "Your Trusted Partner for a Secure Digital Future"
BBCA will be positioned as the epitome of stability, reliability, and secure digital leadership. For at-risk customers, BBCA represents peace of mind, offering a robust and intuitive digital banking experience backed by unparalleled trust and a proven track record. The focus will be on reinforcing its status as the go-to bank for secure, efficient, and dependable financial management in the digital age.

#### BMRI Positioning: "Your Innovative Partner for a Connected Financial Life"
BMRI will be positioned as the dynamic innovator, the "Main Transaction Bank" that empowers customers with cutting-edge, comprehensive, and integrated digital financial solutions. For at-risk customers, BMRI offers a future-forward banking experience that is agile, feature-rich, and designed to seamlessly integrate into their modern, digital lifestyles, making financial management effortless and interconnected.

### 4. Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

#### BBCA UVP:
**"Experience unparalleled security and seamless digital banking with BBCA – where your financial well-being is our priority, and every transaction is backed by Indonesia's most trusted financial institution."**
*   **For Savvy Switchers:** Transparent, competitive pricing with the assurance of a stable, secure platform.
*   **For Digital Dynamos:** A highly reliable and intuitive digital platform with robust security features.
*   **For Prudent Professionals:** The ultimate peace of mind through industry-leading security, reliability, and trusted service.

#### BMRI UVP:
**"Unlock a world of integrated financial possibilities with BMRI – your innovative digital hub for smart, flexible, and connected banking solutions designed to simplify and enrich your modern life."**
*   **For Savvy Switchers:** Value-packed digital bundles and flexible solutions that optimize costs and offer more features.
*   **For Digital Dynamos:** A feature-rich, constantly evolving digital ecosystem with advanced self-service and real-time capabilities.
*   **For Prudent Professionals:** Personalized, proactive digital solutions that offer convenience without compromising on innovation or comprehensive support.

### 5. Messaging Framework

#### Core Message (Overall):
"Reimagine your banking experience. With [BBCA/BMRI], discover tailored solutions that bring you greater value, seamless digital convenience, and unwavering peace of mind."

#### Segment-Specific Messaging:

*   **The Savvy Switcher (Value-Conscious, Price-Sensitive):**
    *   "Get more for less. [BBCA/BMRI] offers transparent pricing and smart bundles designed to maximize your savings and value."
    *   "Stop overpaying. Explore [BBCA/BMRI]'s flexible plans and see how much you can save without compromising on quality."
    *   *Call to Action:* "Switch to a better value plan today!"

*   **The Digital Dynamo (Tech-Savvy, Efficient, Feature-Rich):**
    *   "Your bank, your way. Experience lightning-fast transactions, intuitive apps, and powerful self-service tools with [BBCA/BMRI]."
    *   "Stay ahead with [BBCA/BMRI]'s innovative digital features. Manage everything seamlessly, anytime, anywhere."
    *   *Call to Action:* "Explore our latest digital features!"

*   **The Prudent Professional (Security, Reliability, Trust, Personalized Service):**
    *   "Bank with confidence. [BBCA/BMRI] combines robust security with personalized support, ensuring your financial future is always protected."
    *   "Your trusted financial partner. [BBCA/BMRI] delivers reliable service and expert guidance for your evolving needs."
    *   *Call to Action:* "Connect with your dedicated relationship manager."

#### Churn Driver-Specific Messaging:

*   **Month-to-Month Contract Holders (Goal: Convert to annual/multi-year):**
    *   "Unlock greater savings and benefits! Convert your month-to-month plan to an annual contract with [BBCA/BMRI] and enjoy exclusive rewards/discounts."
    *   "Secure your future, save more. Long-term commitment with [BBCA/BMRI] means more value and stability."
    *   *Offer:* Bonus points, higher interest rates, waived fees for annual commitment.

*   **New Customers (Tenure < 12 months) (Goal: Improve 6-month retention):**
    *   "Welcome to the [BBCA/BMRI] family! Discover all the ways we can support your financial journey. Here's a quick guide to get started."
    *   "We're here to help you thrive. Explore our personalized resources and dedicated support for new customers."
    *   *Offer:* Enhanced welcome program, dedicated onboarding support, first-year loyalty bonuses.

*   **No Tech Support Uptake (Goal: Increase digital tech support usage):**
    *   "Need help? Get instant answers with [BBCA/BMRI]'s in-app chat and comprehensive FAQs. Your digital assistant is always ready."
    *   "Empower yourself with [BBCA/BMRI]'s digital tools. Learn how to manage your account, secure your transactions, and get support, all from your phone."
    *   *Offer:* Gamified tutorials, incentives for first-time digital support usage, easy access buttons.

*   **High Monthly Charges Customers (> IDR 100,000) (Goal: Reduce churn):**
    *   "Are you getting the most value? [BBCA/BMRI] offers premium bundles and personalized solutions designed to optimize your banking experience and reduce overall costs."
    *   "Maximize your benefits. Review your [BBCA/BMRI] plan with our experts to ensure you're on the best-value package for your needs."
    *   *Offer:* Personalized financial review, exclusive premium bundles with added benefits (e.g., higher transaction limits, dedicated support, investment insights).

### 6. Channel Mix

A digital-first, mobile-centric approach will be paramount, complemented by strategic offline touchpoints.

*   **Digital Channels:**
    *   **In-App Notifications & Banners:** Highly targeted, personalized messages based on customer behavior (e.g., M-t-M contract expiry, low tech support usage).
    *   **Email Marketing:** Segmented campaigns for onboarding, value offers, digital feature highlights, and personalized financial reviews.
    *   **SMS/WhatsApp:** Timely alerts, quick tips, and direct links to offers or support.
    *   **Social Media (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn):** Engaging content showcasing digital convenience, security tips, new features, and customer testimonials. Targeted ads for specific segments.
    *   **Paid Digital Advertising (Google Ads, Display Networks):** Retargeting campaigns for at-risk segments, promoting specific offers (e.g., contract conversion, premium bundles).
    *   **Website/Blog:** Dedicated landing pages for offers, comprehensive FAQs, and educational content on digital banking benefits.

*   **Offline Channels (Strategic Support):**
    *   **Branch Network:** For Prudent Professionals seeking personalized consultations, especially for high-charge customers or complex inquiries. Staff trained to promote digital channels.
    *   **Call Center:** Proactive outbound calls for high-value at-risk customers (e.g., high monthly charges, new customers) offering personalized assistance and guiding them to digital solutions. Inbound support for complex issues.

### 7. Creative Direction

#### Tone & Voice:
*   **BBCA:** Authoritative, reassuring, sophisticated, and clear. Emphasizes trust, stability, and seamless efficiency.
*   **BMRI:** Innovative, dynamic, empowering, and friendly. Highlights progress, convenience, and comprehensive solutions.
*   **Overall:** Educational, empathetic, and solution-oriented. Avoid jargon. Focus on benefits, not just features.

#### Visuals:
*   **BBCA:** Clean, modern, professional aesthetics. Use calming blues, whites, and subtle greens. Imagery should convey security (e.g., biometric locks, secure data centers), seamless digital experiences (e.g., smooth app interfaces), and trusted relationships (e.g., diverse individuals confidently using banking services).
*   **BMRI:** Vibrant, energetic, and forward-looking. Use bright, engaging colors (e.g., dynamic blues, purples, oranges). Imagery should depict interconnectedness (e.g., people interacting with multiple digital devices), innovation (e.g., futuristic interfaces, smart solutions), and ease of use (e.g., smiling individuals effortlessly managing finances).
*   **Common Elements:** High-quality, diverse representation of Indonesian customers (25-45 age range). Focus on mobile-first design in all digital assets. Infographics and short videos to explain complex benefits simply.

#### Call to Action (CTA):
Clear, concise, and benefit-driven. Examples:
*   "Upgrade & Save Now"
*   "Experience Seamless Banking"
*   "Secure Your Future"
*   "Chat with Us Instantly"
*   "Discover Your Benefits"
*   "Get Personalized Advice"

### 8. Phased Rollout Plan

#### Phase 1: Foundation & Immediate Impact (Months 1-2)
*   **Focus:** Data analysis refinement, targeted communication setup, and immediate churn reduction for M-t-M contracts and new customers.
*   **Activities:**
    *   **Data Deep Dive:** Refine customer segmentation based on churn propensity scores and specific at-risk indicators.
    *   **Targeted Offers Launch:**
        *   **M-t-M Contracts:** Launch "Upgrade & Save" campaign via in-app, email, and SMS, offering incentives for annual/multi-year conversions.
        *   **New Customers:** Implement enhanced "Welcome & Thrive" program with personalized onboarding emails, in-app guides, and proactive check-ins (SMS/call).
    *   **Digital Support Promotion:** Initiate "Instant Help" campaign via in-app banners and social media, highlighting in-app chat and FAQ benefits with gamified tutorials.
    *   **Internal Training:** Equip call center and branch staff to identify at-risk customers and promote relevant offers/digital channels.
*   **Goals Addressed:** M-t-M conversion, new customer retention, initial digital tech support uptake.

#### Phase 2: Engagement & Deepening Relationships (Months 3-4)
*   **Focus:** Sustained engagement, value demonstration for high-charge customers, and continued digital adoption.
*   **Activities:**
    *   **Value-Added Bundles:** Launch "Maximize Your Value" campaign for high monthly charges customers via email, personalized calls, and branch consultations, offering tailored premium bundles or financial reviews.
    *   **Digital Feature Showcases:** Regular social media campaigns and in-app notifications highlighting specific digital features (e.g., security settings, budgeting tools, QR payments) relevant to Digital Dynamos and Prudent Professionals.
    *   **Loyalty Program Enhancements:** Introduce or enhance loyalty points/rewards for long-term engagement and digital channel usage.
    *   **Feedback Loop:** Implement in-app surveys and direct feedback channels to gather insights from at-risk segments.
*   **Goals Addressed:** High-charge customer churn reduction, increased digital tech support usage, overall churn reduction.

#### Phase 3: Optimization & Long-Term Loyalty (Months 5-6)
*   **Focus:** Continuous improvement, personalized experiences, and fostering long-term loyalty.
*   **Activities:**
    *   **Personalized Financial Insights:** Leverage data to offer proactive, personalized financial advice or product recommendations via in-app or email.
    *   **Community Building:** Create online forums or social media groups for customers to share tips and get support, fostering a sense of belonging.
    *   **Referral Program:** Launch a "Refer a Friend" program with attractive incentives for both referrer and referee, leveraging satisfied customers to attract new ones.
    *   **Performance Review:** Comprehensive review of all campaign metrics against initial goals. Adjust strategies based on performance data and customer feedback.
*   **Goals Addressed:** Sustained overall churn reduction, long-term retention across all segments.

This phased approach ensures a systematic and measurable execution, allowing for continuous optimization towards achieving the ambitious churn reduction and retention goals for both BBCA and BMRI.
