# Internal Campaign Brief: SmartPath Loyalty: Your Intelligent Financial Journey

**Brand:** BBCA & BMRI
**Campaign Type:** Customer Retention

---

## Campaign Name
SmartPath Loyalty: Your Intelligent Financial Journey

## Objective
To position BBCA & BMRI as the "Intelligent Financial Partner for Modern Lifestyles" by significantly improving customer retention and engagement.
*   Reduce overall churn from 17% to 14% within 6 months.
*   Reduce "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2) churn by 25% within 90 days.
*   Increase month-to-month contract conversions by 15%.
*   Boost auto-payment adoption by 20%.

## Target Audience

1.  **Segment 2: "New & Nimble Churners"** (High churn risk, short tenure, month-to-month contracts)
    *   **The Digital Nomad:** Young, tech-savvy, values flexibility, low loyalty, seeks seamless digital experiences.
    *   **The Savvy Family Planner:** Responsible, value-conscious, seeks reliability, sensitive to fees, appreciates good customer service.
2.  **Segment 1: "High-Value Digital Users"** (Fiber optic internet, higher monthly charges)
    *   **The Ambitious Entrepreneur:** Demanding, expects premium service, advanced digital tools, personalized solutions, high-value but conditional loyalty.

## Key Message
**BBCA & BMRI: Your Intelligent Financial Partner for a Modern Lifestyle.** Experience seamless digital solutions, personalized value, and proactive support designed to secure and elevate your financial journey.

*   **For "New & Nimble Churners":** Flexible solutions, seamless digital experiences, and clear value for your evolving life.
*   **For "High-Value Digital Users":** Elevate your financial future with premium service, advanced tools, and proactive, personalized partnership.

## Channels
*   **Digital Platforms:** Mobile App, Website (personalized dashboards), Email, SMS, In-app notifications.
*   **Targeted Advertising:** Social Media (retargeting, lookalike audiences), Search Engine Marketing.
*   **Direct Engagement:** Proactive customer service outreach, personalized offers.
*   **Content Marketing:** Financial literacy resources (webinars, articles, guides).

## Creative Requirements
*   **Visuals:** Modern, clean, intuitive, and digitally sophisticated. Imagery should resonate with the target segments (e.g., tech-savvy individuals, secure families, thriving entrepreneurs).
*   **Tone:** Empowering, intelligent, supportive, reliable, and forward-thinking.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Segment 2:** Focus on ease of use, flexibility, value-added bundles, rewards for loyalty/AutoPay, and clear benefits of long-term commitment.
    *   **Segment 1:** Highlight premium features, personalized financial insights, proactive wealth management, and enhanced digital security.
    *   **Call to Action:** Clear, benefit-driven (e.g., "Switch to AutoPay & Earn Rewards," "Explore Personalized Financial Tools," "Secure Your Long-Term Savings").
*   **Personalization:** Essential across all touchpoints, leveraging data for relevant offers and communications.

## Timeline
*   **Campaign Launch:** Immediate, with continuous optimization.
*   **Initial Focus (90 Days):** Aggressive push for Segment 2 churn reduction and AutoPay adoption.
*   **Mid-Term Focus (6 Months):** Overall churn reduction and long-term contract conversions.

## Budget Split
*   **Digital Engagement & Personalization:** 40%
*   **Incentives & Rewards (Bundles, Welcome Bonuses):** 30%
*   **Content Creation & Financial Literacy:** 15%
*   **Customer Service Training & Proactive Outreach:** 10%
*   **Measurement & Analytics:** 5%

## Success Metrics
*   Overall churn rate reduced from 17% to 14%.
*   Segment 2 churn reduced by 25%.
*   Month-to-month contract conversions increased by 15%.
*   Auto-payment adoption boosted by 20%.
*   Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) increase (secondary metric).
*   Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) scores improvement (secondary metric).
