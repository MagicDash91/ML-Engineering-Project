# Marketing Strategy: Churn Reduction & Customer Loyalty for BBCA & BMRI

## 1. Positioning

BBCA & BMRI will position itself as the **"Intelligent Financial Partner for Modern Lifestyles."** We aim to be recognized as the leading choice for individuals and businesses seeking a seamless, secure, and rewarding financial experience that adapts to their evolving needs.

*   **For "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2):** We are the flexible and value-driven partner that empowers smart financial choices, offering effortless digital solutions and rewarding loyalty from day one. We understand the need for adaptability while providing the stability and benefits of a long-term relationship.
*   **For "High-Value Digital Users" (Segment 1):** We are the premium financial ecosystem that fuels ambition, providing advanced digital tools, personalized solutions, and exclusive support to elevate entrepreneurial success and maximize financial growth.

This positioning emphasizes both the practical benefits (value, ease, security) and the aspirational outcomes (growth, empowerment, rewards), catering to the distinct motivations of our target segments while maintaining a unified brand promise of intelligent financial management.

## 2. Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

Our core UVP is **"Seamless Financial Empowerment: Maximize Your Value, Effortlessly."** We offer a superior blend of digital convenience, personalized value, and robust security, ensuring customers can manage their finances intelligently and achieve their goals with ease.

*   **For "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2):**
    *   **"Smart Savings, Simplified Living."** We offer transparent, value-packed long-term plans and hassle-free digital tools (like AutoPay) that put more money back in your pocket and free up your time. Experience the freedom of flexibility combined with the rewards of commitment, making your financial journey both easy and profitable.
*   **For "High-Value Digital Users" (Segment 1):**
    *   **"Amplify Your Ambition with Premium Digital Finance."** Gain access to an exclusive suite of advanced digital financial tools, personalized advisory services, and a loyalty program designed to accelerate your growth and optimize your financial efficiency. We provide the sophisticated support and solutions your high-value endeavors demand.

This dual UVP ensures that while the overarching brand message is consistent, the specific benefits highlighted resonate deeply with the unique pain points and aspirations of each segment.

## 4. Channel Mix

A multi-channel approach will ensure maximum reach and engagement, leveraging digital for efficiency and direct channels for personalized impact.

*   **Digital Channels (Primary for all segments):**
    *   **In-App Notifications & Banners:** Highly targeted, real-time offers (e.g., "Upgrade & Hemat," "AutoPay Anti-Ribet"). Ideal for immediate action.
    *   **Email Marketing:** Personalized campaigns with detailed benefits, FAQs, and clear CTAs. Segmented lists for tailored content (e.g., "Welcome Bonus" for new customers, loyalty tiers for Segment 1).
    *   **SMS/WhatsApp Marketing:** Short, urgent reminders and exclusive flash offers, especially for Segment 2 to prevent churn.
    *   **Social Media (Paid & Organic):**
        *   **Facebook/Instagram:** Engaging visuals and benefit-driven ads for Segment 2 ("Digital Nomad," "Savvy Family Planner").
        *   **LinkedIn/Google Ads:** Targeted ads for Segment 1 ("Ambitious Entrepreneur") promoting advanced features and loyalty benefits.
    *   **Website Landing Pages:** Dedicated, optimized pages for each campaign with clear value propositions and conversion forms.
    *   **Retargeting Ads:** To re-engage users who visited campaign pages but didn't convert.
*   **Direct Channels (For personalized engagement & high-risk customers):**
    *   **Customer Service/Call Center:** Proactive outbound calls to identified high-churn-risk Segment 2 customers with personalized offers. Inbound agents trained to upsell long-term contracts and AutoPay during service inquiries.
    *   **Branch Network:** In-branch promotions, digital kiosks, and trained staff to educate customers on benefits of long-term contracts, AutoPay, and loyalty programs. Especially relevant for "Savvy Family Planner" who may prefer face-to-face interaction.
    *   **Direct Mail (Selective):** For high-value Segment 1 customers or specific loyalty program announcements, offering a premium touch.

## 6. Phased Rollout Plan

This phased approach prioritizes immediate churn reduction for Segment 2, followed by contract conversion and comprehensive loyalty building, while continuously monitoring and optimizing.

### Phase 1: Foundation & Immediate Churn Reduction (Months 1-2)

*   **Goal:** Reduce churn in Segment 2 by 25% within 90 days; increase AutoPay adoption.
*   **Campaigns:**
    1.  **"Welcome Bonus: Setia Sejak Awal"**:
        *   **Target:** New customers (tenure < 3 months) identified as high-risk.
        *   **Offer:** Waived fees, bonus points, or small cashback for setting up AutoPay or committing to a 12/24-month contract within the first 30 days.
        *   **Channels:** Onboarding emails, in-app notifications, SMS during initial setup.
    2.  **"AutoPay Anti-Ribet: Cashback Tiap Bulan"**:
        *   **Target:** All customers currently using manual payment methods, with a strong focus on Segment 2.
        *   **Offer:** Cashback for activating AutoPay.
        *   **Channels:** In-app banners, email campaigns, SMS reminders, customer service prompts, social media ads.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Utilize predictive churn model to identify and prioritize high-risk Segment 2 customers for proactive outreach.
    *   Train customer service teams on new offers and churn prevention scripts.
    *   Launch targeted digital advertising campaigns for both initiatives.
    *   A/B test different messaging and offer variations to optimize conversion.
*   **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Segment 2 churn rate, AutoPay adoption rate, new customer long-term contract conversion rate.

### Phase 2: Contract Conversion & Value Reinforcement (Months 3-4)

*   **Goal:** Increase conversion rate from month-to-month to 12/24-month contracts by 15%; further reduce Segment 2 churn.
*   **Campaigns:**
    1.  **"Upgrade & Hemat: Kontrak Tahunan Lebih Untung"**:
        *   **Target:** Existing month-to-month Segment 2 customers.
        *   **Offer:** Significant discounts, enhanced cashback, or exclusive benefits for upgrading to a 12/24-month contract.
        *   **Channels:** Personalized email campaigns, in-app offers, outbound calls from customer service, retargeting ads.
    2.  **Continue "AutoPay Anti-Ribet"**: Refresh messaging and target non-adopters.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Develop educational content (e.g., blog posts, short videos) highlighting the long-term financial benefits and savings of annual contracts.
    *   Run social media campaigns showcasing customer testimonials about savings from long-term contracts.
    *   Analyze Phase 1 results to refine targeting and optimize campaign spend.
*   **KPIs:** Month-to-month to long-term contract conversion rate, Segment 2 churn rate, customer lifetime value (CLTV) for converted customers.

### Phase 3: Loyalty & High-Value Retention (Months 5-6)

*   **Goal:** Reduce overall customer churn rate from 17% to 14%; enhance Segment 1 retention and loyalty.
*   **Campaigns:**
    1.  **"BBCA/BMRI Loyalty Rewards: Makin Setia, Makin Untung"**:
        *   **Target:** All customers, with a tiered structure that heavily rewards Segment 1 and long-tenure Segment 2 customers.
        *   **Offer:** Exclusive access to premium features, dedicated support, higher cashback rates, personalized financial advisory, and special event invitations.
        *   **Channels:** Personalized email, in-app announcements, direct mail for top tiers, dedicated loyalty program landing page, customer relationship managers (for Segment 1).
*   **Activities:**
    *   Launch the comprehensive loyalty program with clear tiers and benefits.
    *   Host exclusive webinars or online events for Segment 1 on advanced financial strategies and tools.
    *   Gather feedback from loyalty program members to continuously enhance offerings.
    *   Conduct a comprehensive review of overall churn rate and progress towards the 14% goal.
*   **KPIs:** Overall churn rate, Segment 1 retention rate, loyalty program engagement metrics (e.g., tier progression, feature adoption), customer satisfaction scores.

### Measurement & Optimization:

Throughout all phases, continuous monitoring and data-driven optimization will be paramount. We will leverage our predictive churn model (0.92 AUC-ROC) for proactive interventions, conduct regular A/B testing on all campaign elements, and gather customer feedback to ensure the strategy remains agile and effective in achieving our ambitious goals.
