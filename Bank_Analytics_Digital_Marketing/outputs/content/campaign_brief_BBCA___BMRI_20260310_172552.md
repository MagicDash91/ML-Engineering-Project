# Internal Campaign Brief: FutureForward Banking: Stay & Thrive

**Campaign Name:** FutureForward Banking: Stay & Thrive

**Objective:**
This campaign aims to significantly reduce customer churn among at-risk segments for BBCA & BMRI by demonstrating enhanced value, digital convenience, and trusted security.
*   Reduce overall customer churn from 26.5% to 20% within 6 months.
*   Increase month-to-month contract conversions by 15% within 90 days.
*   Improve new customer retention (tenure < 12 months) by 10%.
*   Increase digital tech support usage by 20% within 90 days.
*   Reduce churn among high monthly charges customers (> IDR 100,000) by 8% within 6 months.

**Target Audience:**
At-risk banking customers in Indonesia (25-45 years old), segmented into three key personas:

1.  **The Savvy Switcher:** Value-conscious, price-sensitive, seeking transparent pricing and cost minimization.
    *   *Specific At-Risk Groups:* Month-to-Month Contract Holders, High Monthly Charges Customers (> IDR 100,000).
2.  **The Digital Dynamo:** Tech-savvy, prioritizing seamless, efficient, and feature-rich digital banking with self-service options.
    *   *Specific At-Risk Groups:* Customers with No Tech Support Uptake.
3.  **The Prudent Professional:** Established individuals valuing security, reliability, trust, and personalized service.
    *   *Specific At-Risk Groups:* New Customers (Tenure < 12 months), High Monthly Charges Customers (> IDR 100,000).

**Key Message:**
"Experience unparalleled value, seamless digital banking, and trusted security tailored to your financial journey with BBCA & BMRI. Stay with us and thrive."

**Channels:**
*   **Digital:** Email marketing, In-app notifications, SMS campaigns, Targeted social media ads (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn), Website banners, Blog posts, Tutorial videos.
*   **Direct:** Personalized outbound calls (for high-value/at-risk segments), Branch staff engagement and training, Direct mail (for specific offers).
*   **In-Branch:** Point-of-sale materials, Staff training for proactive retention conversations.

**Creative Requirements:**
*   **Overall:** Professional, modern, trustworthy, and localized visuals reflecting Indonesian culture and financial aspirations. Consistent branding for both BBCA & BMRI where applicable.
*   **The Savvy Switcher:** Clear, concise messaging highlighting cost savings, transparent fees, and tangible benefits. Visuals of smart financial choices, growth, and value (e.g., for 'Upgrade & Hemat', 'Smart Savings Bundle').
*   **The Digital Dynamo:** Dynamic, clean design emphasizing ease of use, speed, and innovative digital features. Visuals of intuitive app interfaces, quick transactions, and mobile-first experiences (e.g., for 'Digital Care Connect').
*   **The Prudent Professional:** Sophisticated and reassuring visuals conveying security, reliability, and personalized expert support. Imagery of stability, professional guidance, and peace of mind (e.g., for 'Welcome Bonus', personalized service).

**Timeline:**
*   **Month 1-2 (Launch & Awareness):** Initial rollout of 'Welcome Bonus' and 'Upgrade & Hemat' campaigns. Broad digital outreach.
*   **Month 3-4 (Engagement & Support):** Focus on 'Digital Care Connect' and personalized outreach for high-risk segments. Reinforce value propositions.
*   **Month 5-6 (Optimization & Retention):** Launch 'Smart Savings Bundle'. Analyze initial results, refine messaging, and intensify efforts for remaining at-risk customers. Ongoing monitoring and reporting.

**Budget Split (Illustrative):**
*   Digital Advertising (Social, Search, Display): 40%
*   Content Creation & Creative Assets: 25%
*   Personalized Direct Outreach (CRM, Call Center): 20%
*   Internal Training & Branch Support: 10%
*   Analytics & Contingency: 5%

**Success Metrics:**
*   Overall customer churn rate (target: 20%).
*   Month-to-month contract conversion rate (target: +15%).
*   New customer retention rate (tenure < 12 months) (target: +10%).
*   Digital tech support usage rate (target: +20%).
*   Churn rate among high monthly charges customers (> IDR 100,000) (target: -8%).
*   Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) for retained customers.
*   Net Promoter Score (NPS) for targeted segments.
