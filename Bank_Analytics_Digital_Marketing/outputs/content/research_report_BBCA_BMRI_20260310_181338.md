## 1. Executive Summary

This report provides comprehensive market research to inform the digital marketing campaign for BBCA & BMRI, focusing on customer churn prevention and retention. It includes an analysis of the competitive landscape, detailed audience personas for at-risk banking customers, current industry trends, and emerging opportunities in retention marketing. The insights aim to support the campaign's goal of reducing churn by 10-15% in targeted segments within the next 6 months, preserving over $2.1M in annual revenue.

## 2. Industry Trends and Market Size

The Indonesian retail banking sector is experiencing significant transformation, driven by digital adoption and the rise of alternative finance.

*   **Market Size:** The retail core banking solutions market in Indonesia is valued at approximately **USD 1.4 billion**, with significant growth fueled by digital banking adoption. The alternative lending market is projected to reach **US$6.60 billion by 2025**. The broader Indonesian retail market is expected to grow by **USD 49.9 billion from 2025-2029**.
*   **Loan Growth:** Consumption loan growth is a significant contributor to overall loan expansion.

**Top 5 Emerging Trends in Indonesian Retail Banking:**

1.  **Accelerated Digital Banking Adoption:** The primary driver for growth in retail core banking solutions.
2.  **Explosive Growth in Alternative Lending:** Rapidly gaining traction, addressing unmet credit needs.
3.  **Strategic Integration of AI:** Leveraging AI for market pricing discipline, customer value, precision growth, and AI operations.
4.  **Expansion of the Retail Landscape:** Increased consumer spending and commercial activity creating demand for integrated banking solutions.
5.  **Consumption-Driven Loan Demand:** Individual consumer spending and lifestyle needs increasingly driving credit demand.

**Technology Disruptions:** Digital banking platforms, AI, Fintech in alternative lending, and core banking modernization are key disruptors.

**Consumer Behaviour Shifts:** Preference for digital channels, openness to alternative credit, increased propensity for consumption-based borrowing, and expectation of personalized value.

**Untapped Opportunities:** Hyper-personalized financial solutions, embedded finance in retail ecosystems, expanding alternative lending to underserved segments, digital financial literacy & inclusion, and data monetization and insights.

## 5. Top 5 Emerging Trends for Retention Marketing in Banking (2024-2025)

1.  **Hyper-Personalization with AI:** Leveraging Generative AI to create deeply personalized, proactive, and emotionally engaging experiences that drive loyalty and retention. This moves beyond basic segmentation to individual-level customization.
2.  **Embedded Finance and Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS):** Integrating banking services seamlessly into non-financial platforms and daily activities, making financial interactions more convenient and less intrusive. This can increase stickiness by making the bank an invisible, yet essential, part of the customer's ecosystem.
3.  **Focus on Financial Wellness & Advisory:** Shifting from transactional relationships to offering proactive financial guidance, tools, and education. This builds deeper trust and positions the bank as a partner in the customer's financial journey, especially for those with high monthly charges or new adopters needing guidance.
4.  **Seamless Omnichannel Experience:** Ensuring consistent and high-quality customer experience across all touchpoints – digital, mobile, and physical. This is crucial for customers who might be hesitant about digital-only interactions (like the Cautious Adopter) or those who expect efficiency across all channels (like the Discerning Professional).
5.  **Proactive Problem Resolution & Tech Support:** Utilizing data and AI to anticipate customer issues and offer solutions before they escalate. For segments lacking tech support usage, this means making support easily accessible, intuitive, and rewarding, potentially through in-app chat, video tutorials, or gamified learning.

## 6. Strategic Opportunities for BBCA & BMRI

Based on the research, here are strategic opportunities to enhance customer retention:

1.  **AI-Driven Personalized Onboarding & Engagement:** For "New Adopters" (Segment 0) and new customers, implement AI-powered onboarding journeys that proactively offer relevant tips, feature highlights, and support based on their initial usage patterns. This directly addresses the "Tenure" churn driver.
2.  **Value-Based Contract Conversion Programs:** For month-to-month contract holders, move beyond simple discounts. Offer personalized value propositions for annual contracts, highlighting long-term savings, exclusive benefits (e.g., higher interest rates, premium support), and financial planning tools. This addresses the "Contract Type" churn driver.
3.  **Gamified Digital Adoption & Tech Support:** For customers with low tech support usage and those using non-electronic payment methods, introduce gamified experiences within the mobile app. Reward them for activating 2FA, setting up auto-debit, using digital payment methods, or successfully resolving issues via in-app chat. This tackles "Payment Method" and "Tech Support Usage" drivers.
4.  **Proactive Financial Health Reviews for High-Value Customers:** For customers with high monthly charges (>$90), offer personalized financial health check-ups. Leverage AI to identify potential cost savings or better-suited products, and provide access to financial advisors. This addresses the "Monthly Charges" churn driver by demonstrating value.
5.  **Community & Educational Content Hubs:** Develop digital content hubs (e.g., blog, video series, webinars) focused on financial literacy, digital banking tips, and security best practices. Tailor content to address the pain points and motivations of each persona, especially the "Cautious Adopter" who needs clear guidance.
6.  **Seamless Cross-Channel Support:** Ensure that customer service interactions are consistent and efficient across all channels (in-app chat, WhatsApp, phone, branch). For complex issues, allow customers to seamlessly transition from digital to human support without repeating information.
