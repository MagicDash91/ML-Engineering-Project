## Marketing Strategy: Proactive Retention for BBCA & BMRI

**Executive Summary:**
This strategy outlines a comprehensive approach for BBCA & BMRI to proactively address customer churn among at-risk segments, leveraging an ML-driven identification model. By focusing on personalized engagement, digital empowerment, and value optimization, we aim to reduce overall churn from 26.5% to 22% within 9 months, significantly impact 'New Adopters' churn, convert month-to-month contracts, increase digital service activation, and drive electronic payment adoption, ultimately preserving over $2.1M in annual revenue.

---

### 1. Positioning

**BBCA & BMRI: Your Proactive Partner for a Secure and Empowered Financial Journey.**

We position BBCA & BMRI not merely as banking service providers, but as indispensable partners who understand and anticipate our customers' evolving financial needs. We are committed to providing a seamless, secure, and personalized banking experience that empowers individuals to achieve financial independence and peace of mind. Our strength lies in combining digital innovation with human-centric support, ensuring every customer, regardless of their digital comfort level, feels valued, understood, and supported.

---

### 2. Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

**"Personalized Proactive Support & Seamless Digital Empowerment for Your Financial Growth."**

Our UVP centers on:
*   **Proactive Personalization:** Leveraging advanced ML insights to anticipate customer needs and potential churn, delivering tailored solutions and support before issues arise.
*   **Seamless Digital & Human Touch:** Offering intuitive digital tools for convenience and efficiency, complemented by accessible, empathetic human guidance for those who need it, ensuring a truly omnichannel experience.
*   **Empowering Financial Wellness:** Providing resources, insights, and services that not only meet transactional needs but also foster long-term financial health and confidence.

This UVP directly addresses the audience's values of financial independence, transparency, convenience, efficiency, security, and personalized value, while mitigating key churn drivers through proactive engagement.

### 4. Channel Mix

Our channel strategy will be omnichannel, prioritizing digital channels for efficiency and personalization, while maintaining crucial human touchpoints.

*   **Digital Channels (Primary):**
    *   **In-App Notifications & Messaging:** Highly personalized alerts, tips, offers, and direct links to support or feature activation. Ideal for 'New Adopters' and digital service activation.
    *   **Email Marketing:** Segmented campaigns for onboarding, contract conversion, payment method adoption, and value reviews. Personalized content based on ML insights.
    *   **SMS/WhatsApp:** Timely, concise reminders, quick tips, and direct links for urgent actions or support.
    *   **Social Media (Paid & Organic):** Targeted ads for awareness and engagement, educational content (e.g., "How-to" videos for digital services), community building, and customer service.
    *   **Website/Landing Pages:** Dedicated pages for campaign offers, FAQs, digital service guides, and financial wellness content.
    *   **Targeted Digital Ads:** Programmatic advertising on relevant platforms, retargeting at-risk segments with specific value propositions.

*   **Direct Channels (Support & Conversion):**
    *   **Call Center/Customer Service:** Proactive outbound calls to high-value at-risk customers (e.g., 'Premium Value Review'), inbound support for digital service adoption and issue resolution. Staff training on churn drivers and retention scripts.
    *   **Branch Network:** In-branch consultations for complex issues, digital literacy workshops, and personalized assistance for customers preferring traditional methods (e.g., 'Cautious Adopter' persona).
    *   **Relationship Managers:** For high-value customers, personalized outreach and financial health reviews.

*   **Content & PR:**
    *   **Financial Wellness Blog/Articles:** Educational content on budgeting, digital security, maximizing banking benefits, and financial planning.
    *   **Webinars/Online Workshops:** Interactive sessions on using digital tools, understanding contracts, and secure banking practices.

### 6. Phased Rollout Plan

**Phase 1: Foundation & Immediate Impact (Months 1-3)**
*   **Focus:** Address immediate churn risks: 'New Adopters' and Month-to-Month contract holders. Establish proactive support for new customers.
*   **Campaigns Launched:**
    *   **'BBCA/BMRI Welcome Bonus: Digital Starter Pack'**: (Target: New Adopters, New Customers, Lacking Tech Support)
        *   **Activities:** Personalized onboarding emails/in-app messages, welcome calls, guided tours of digital services, small incentives for first digital transaction/bill pay.
        *   **Channels:** In-app, Email, SMS, Call Center.
    *   **'Setia & Hemat: Upgrade Tahunan!'**: (Target: Month-to-Month Contract Holders)
        *   **Activities:** Targeted email/in-app offers highlighting savings and benefits of annual contracts, personalized calls from relationship managers, dedicated landing page.
        *   **Channels:** Email, In-app, Call Center, Website.
*   **Metrics:** 'New Adopters' churn rate, Month-to-Month conversion rate, digital service activation for new customers.

**Phase 2: Deepening Engagement & Digital Adoption (Months 4-6)**
*   **Focus:** Drive adoption of electronic payments, enhance tech support usage, and provide value for high-charge customers.
*   **Campaigns Launched:**
    *   **'SmartPay Rewards: Auto-Debit & Earn'**: (Target: Non-Electronic Check Payment Users)
        *   **Activities:** Targeted email/SMS campaigns promoting auto-debit/electronic payments with reward incentives (cashback, points), in-app guides for setup.
        *   **Channels:** Email, SMS, In-app, Social Media.
    *   **'Digital Guardian: Secure & Supported'**: (Target: Lacking Tech Support Usage)
        *   **Activities:** Proactive in-app tips on using tech support features, short video tutorials, dedicated helpline promotion, "Digital Literacy" webinars.
        *   **Channels:** In-app, Email, Social Media, Website, Webinars.
    *   **'Premium Value Review: Optimize Your Plan'**: (Target: Customers with High Monthly Charges)
        *   **Activities:** Personalized outreach (email/call) offering a review of their banking plan to ensure optimal value, highlighting premium benefits and efficiency tools.
        *   **Channels:** Email, Call Center, Relationship Managers.
*   **Metrics:** Electronic payment adoption rate, tech support usage rate, retention rate for high-charge customers, digital service activation among low-engagement customers.

**Phase 3: Optimization & Sustained Growth (Months 7-9)**
*   **Focus:** Refine strategies based on performance data, expand personalized offers, and reinforce long-term customer loyalty.
*   **Activities:**
    *   **Data-Driven Refinement:** Analyze campaign performance, A/B test messaging and offers, and adjust targeting based on ML model updates.
    *   **Personalized Financial Wellness Content:** Deliver tailored financial advice, budgeting tools, and investment insights based on customer profiles and behaviors.
    *   **Community & Loyalty Programs:** Explore gamification for digital service usage, referral programs, and exclusive content for loyal customers.
    *   **Omnichannel Feedback Loop:** Implement continuous feedback mechanisms across all channels to identify emerging pain points and opportunities.
*   **Metrics:** Overall churn rate, sustained improvements in all targeted churn drivers, customer satisfaction scores, revenue preservation.

This phased approach ensures a strategic, measurable, and adaptable marketing effort to significantly reduce churn and enhance customer lifetime value for BBCA & BMRI.
