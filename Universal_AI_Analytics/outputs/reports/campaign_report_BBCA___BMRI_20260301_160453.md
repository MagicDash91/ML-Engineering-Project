# Marketing Strategy: Churn Reduction & Customer Loyalty for BBCA & BMRI

## 1. Positioning

BBCA & BMRI will position itself as the **"Intelligent Financial Partner for Modern Lifestyles."** We aim to be recognized as the leading choice for individuals and businesses seeking a seamless, secure, and rewarding financial experience that adapts to their evolving needs.

*   **For "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2):** We are the flexible and value-driven partner that empowers smart financial choices, offering effortless digital solutions and rewarding loyalty from day one. We understand the need for adaptability while providing the stability and benefits of a long-term relationship.
*   **For "High-Value Digital Users" (Segment 1):** We are the premium financial ecosystem that fuels ambition, providing advanced tools, exclusive insights, and proactive support to elevate their financial journey.

## 2. Target Audience

Our strategy targets two primary segments identified as critical for churn prevention:

1.  **Segment 2: "New & Nimble Churners"**
    *   **Description:** High churn risk, short tenure, month-to-month contracts. Highly susceptible to switching banks due to perceived lack of value, better offers elsewhere, or initial dissatisfaction.
    *   **Personas:**
        *   **The Digital Nomad:** Young, tech-savvy professional (25-30 years old, IDR 8-15M income), values flexibility, convenience, seamless digital experiences, low loyalty. Pain points: slow apps, hidden fees, lack of instant customer support.
        *   **The Savvy Family Planner:** Responsible individual (32-40 years old, IDR 12-25M income), focused on financial security, pragmatic, value-conscious, sensitive to fees. Pain points: high fees, unclear product information, inconsistent customer service.

2.  **Segment 1: "High-Value Digital Users"**
    *   **Description:** High-value customers with fiber optic internet and higher MonthlyCharges. Their retention is paramount as their churn significantly impacts revenue.
    *   **Persona:**
        *   **The Ambitious Entrepreneur:** High-achieving individual (30-45 years old, IDR 25M+ income), demanding, expects premium service, advanced digital tools, personalized solutions, high-value but conditional loyalty.

## 3. Campaign Objectives & KPIs

Our retention strategy is built around measurable outcomes:

### Primary Objectives & KPIs:

1.  **Reduce Overall Customer Churn Rate**
    *   **KPI:** Overall Customer Churn Rate
    *   **Baseline:** 17%
    *   **Target:** 14% (within 6 months)
    *   **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly, Quarterly
    *   **Data Source:** Customer database, churn prediction model outputs

2.  **Reduce Churn within "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2)**
    *   **KPI:** Churn Rate for "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2)
    *   **Baseline:** (Current Segment 2 Churn Rate - to be established, but assumed higher than overall)
    *   **Target:** 25% reduction from baseline Segment 2 churn rate (within 90 days)
    *   **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
    *   **Data Source:** Customer segmentation data, churn prediction model outputs

3.  **Increase Month-to-Month Contract Conversions**
    *   **KPI:** Month-to-Month to 12/24-Month Contract Conversion Rate
    *   **Baseline:** (Current conversion rate - to be established)
    *   **Target:** 15% increase from baseline conversion rate
    *   **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
    *   **Data Source:** CRM, product subscription data, campaign tracking

4.  **Increase Automatic Payment Method Adoption**
    *   **KPI:** Automatic Payment Method Adoption Rate (among customers currently using manual payment methods)
    *   **Baseline:** (Current adoption rate - to be established)
    *   **Target:** 20% increase from baseline adoption rate
    *   **Measurement Frequency:** Monthly
    *   **Data Source:** Payment method data, campaign tracking

### Secondary KPIs:

*   **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):** Increase in CLTV for targeted segments over 6 months.
*   **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) / Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Improvement in scores, particularly for Segment 2 and Segment 1.
*   **Engagement Rates:** Email open rates, click-through rates, in-app feature usage, social media engagement, content consumption (webinars, articles).
*   **Cost Per Retention (CPR):** To optimize the efficiency of retention efforts.

## 4. Key Initiatives & Campaign Ideas

Our strategy leverages data-driven insights and industry trends to implement targeted campaigns:

1.  **Deepen Digital Personalization for "New & Nimble Churners"**
    *   **Campaign:** "Welcome Bonus: Setia Sejak Awal"
    *   **Mechanic:** For new customers (tenure < 3 months), offer a IDR 50,000 welcome bonus or waived transaction fees for 1 month if they set up an automatic payment method or commit to a specific long-term product within their first 60 days.
    *   **Target Driver:** Tenure (shorter tenure), Payment Method.
    *   **Channels:** In-App Notifications/Banners, Personalized Email, SMS/WhatsApp Blasts.

2.  **Incentivize Long-Term Commitment through Value-Added Bundles**
    *   **Campaign:** "Upgrade & Hemat: Kontrak Tahunan Lebih Untung"
    *   **Mechanic:** Offer a 3-month discount on administrative fees or a 5% cashback bonus (up to IDR 50,000) for customers who upgrade from a month-to-month contract to a 12-month or 24-month contract. Enhance with exclusive lifestyle benefits (e.g., streaming discounts, e-commerce vouchers).
    *   **Target Driver:** Contract Type (Month-to-month).
    *   **Channels:** In-App Notifications/Banners, Personalized Email, Social Media Retargeting, Dedicated Landing Pages.

3.  **Integrate "AutoPay" with Lifestyle Rewards**
    *   **Campaign:** "AutoPay Anti-Ribet: Cashback Tiap Bulan"
    *   **Mechanic:** Provide a 5% cashback on monthly charges (up to IDR 25,000) for the first 3 months when customers activate automatic payment for their BBCA/BMRI services. Expand to offer loyalty points redeemable for popular lifestyle purchases (food delivery, ride-hailing).
    *   **Target Driver:** Payment Method (incentivize automatic payments).
    *   **Channels:** SMS/WhatsApp Blasts, In-App Notifications, Website Banners, Google Search Ads, Broad Social Media Campaigns.

4.  **Elevate "High-Value Digital Users" with Proactive Wealth & Digital Security**
    *   **Campaign:** "Digital Pro Upgrade: Keamanan & Kenyamanan Ekstra"
    *   **Mechanic:** Offer a free 3-month trial of premium digital security features (e.g., advanced fraud protection, dedicated tech support hotline) or exclusive early access to new digital banking features. Combine with proactive, personalized financial advisory services (investment insights, tax planning tips).
    *   **Target Driver:** Monthly Charges (enhance perceived value for high spenders).
    *   **Channels:** Personalized Email, In-App Messaging/Push Notifications, Dedicated Landing Pages, Relationship Manager Outreach.

5.  **Proactive Value Enhancement & Loyalty Programs**
    *   **Campaign:** "BBCA/BMRI Loyalty Rewards: Makin Setia, Makin Untung"
    *   **Mechanic:** Launch a tiered loyalty points program where customers earn points based on their MonthlyCharges and tenure. Points can be redeemed for cashback, discounts on bank products, or partner merchant vouchers. Special bonus points for customers with tenure > 12 months or MonthlyCharges > IDR 500,000.
    *   **Target Driver:** Monthly Charges (add value), Tenure (reward loyalty).
    *   **Channels:** Personalized Email, In-App Messaging, Website Banners, Google Search Ads.

## 5. Channel Strategy

Our channel strategy is tailored to each segment's preferences and the campaign's objectives:

*   **For Segment 2 ("New & Nimble Churners"):**
    *   **High Priority:** In-App Notifications/Banners, Personalized Email Marketing, SMS/WhatsApp Blasts, Social Media Retargeting (Facebook, Instagram).
    *   **Purpose:** Direct, immediate, and personalized communication to drive early engagement and contract/payment conversions.

*   **For Segment 1 ("High-Value Digital Users"):**
    *   **High Priority:** Personalized Email Marketing (exclusive invitations), In-App Messaging/Push Notifications (premium features), Dedicated Landing Pages, Relationship Manager Outreach (for top-tier clients).
    *   **Purpose:** Deliver exclusive, value-driven content and personalized service to reinforce loyalty and justify high monthly charges.

*   **For General Churn Drivers (Monthly Charges, Payment Method):**
    *   **Supporting Channels:** Website Banners & Landing Pages, Google Search Ads (targeting relevant keywords), Broad Social Media Campaigns (awareness of loyalty programs, auto-pay benefits).
    *   **Purpose:** Broaden reach for general benefits and capture intent.

# Internal Campaign Brief: SmartPath Loyalty: Your Intelligent Financial Journey

**Brand:** BBCA & BMRI
**Campaign Type:** Customer Retention

---

## Campaign Name
SmartPath Loyalty: Your Intelligent Financial Journey

## Objective
To position BBCA & BMRI as the "Intelligent Financial Partner for Modern Lifestyles" by significantly improving customer retention and engagement.
*   Reduce overall churn from 17% to 14% within 6 months.
*   Reduce "New & Nimble Churners" (Segment 2) churn by 25% within 90 days.
*   Increase month-to-month contract conversions by 15%.
*   Boost auto-payment adoption by 20%.

## Target Audience

1.  **Segment 2: "New & Nimble Churners"** (High churn risk, short tenure, month-to-month contracts)
    *   **The Digital Nomad:** Young, tech-savvy, values flexibility, low loyalty, seeks seamless digital experiences.
    *   **The Savvy Family Planner:** Responsible, value-conscious, seeks reliability, sensitive to fees, appreciates good customer service.
2.  **Segment 1: "High-Value Digital Users"** (Fiber optic internet, higher monthly charges)
    *   **The Ambitious Entrepreneur:** Demanding, expects premium service, advanced digital tools, personalized solutions, high-value but conditional loyalty.

## Key Message
**BBCA & BMRI: Your Intelligent Financial Partner for a Modern Lifestyle.** Experience seamless digital solutions, personalized value, and proactive support designed to secure and elevate your financial journey.

*   **For "New & Nimble Churners":** Flexible solutions, seamless digital experiences, and clear value for your evolving life.
*   **For "High-Value Digital Users":** Elevate your financial future with premium service, advanced tools, and proactive, personalized partnership.

## Channels
*   **Digital Platforms:** Mobile App, Website (personalized dashboards), Email, SMS, In-app notifications.
*   **Targeted Advertising:** Social Media (retargeting, lookalike audiences), Search Engine Marketing.
*   **Direct Engagement:** Proactive customer service outreach, personalized offers.
*   **Content Marketing:** Financial literacy resources (webinars, articles, guides).

## Creative Requirements
*   **Visuals:** Modern, clean, intuitive, and digitally sophisticated. Imagery should resonate with the target segments (e.g., tech-savvy individuals, secure families, thriving entrepreneurs).
*   **Tone:** Empowering, intelligent, supportive, reliable, and forward-thinking.
*   **Content:**
    *   **Segment 2:** Focus on ease of use, flexibility, value-added bundles, rewards for loyalty/AutoPay, and clear benefits of long-term commitment.
    *   **Segment 1:** Highlight premium features, personalized financial insights, proactive wealth management, and enhanced digital security.
    *   **Call to Action:** Clear, benefit-driven (e.g., "Switch to AutoPay & Earn Rewards," "Explore Personalized Financial Tools," "Secure Your Long-Term Savings").
*   **Personalization:** Essential across all touchpoints, leveraging data for relevant offers and communications.

## Timeline
*   **Campaign Launch:** Immediate, with continuous optimization.
*   **Initial Focus (90 Days):** Aggressive push for Segment 2 churn reduction and AutoPay adoption.
*   **Mid-Term Focus (6 Months):** Overall churn reduction and long-term contract conversions.

## Budget Split
*   **Digital Engagement & Personalization:** 40%
*   **Incentives & Rewards (Bundles, Welcome Bonuses):** 30%
*   **Content Creation & Financial Literacy:** 15%
*   **Customer Service Training & Proactive Outreach:** 10%
*   **Measurement & Analytics:** 5%

## Success Metrics
*   Overall churn rate reduced from 17% to 14%.
*   Segment 2 churn reduced by 25%.
*   Month-to-month contract conversions increased by 15%.
*   Auto-payment adoption boosted by 20%.
*   Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) increase (secondary metric).
*   Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) scores improvement (secondary metric).
