# Churn Prevention Strategy Report

## Executive Summary
* **Objective**: Identify high-risk customer segments and propose targeted churn prevention strategies.
* **Key Outcome**: Potential retention of **$1.2M** in annual revenue by reducing churn rate by 15%.
* **Focus Areas**: Enhanced Customer Service, Personalized Offers, and Streamlined Digital Banking

## Key Findings
* **Churn Rate**: Current rate stands at **3.8%** (up 1.2% YoY), affecting **12,456** customers in the last quarter.
* **High-Risk Segments**:
	+ **Demographic**: Customers aged 25-34 (**4.5%** churn rate, 20% of total churned customers).
	+ **Product Usage**: Inactive Credit Card holders (**6.1%** churn rate, 30% of total churned customers).
* **Top Reason for Churn** (from survey data): **Poor Customer Service** (cited by **62%** of churned customers).

## Risk Assessment
* **Financial Risk**: Projected loss of **$2.5M** in revenue if churn rate increases by another 2% without intervention.
* **Reputational Risk**: **4/5** stars rating on review platforms at risk due to negative feedback from churned customers.
* **Risk Priority Matrix**:
	+ **High**: Customer Service Improvement
	+ **Medium**: Personalized Offer Development
	+ **Low**: Digital Banking Enhancement (already showing positive trends)

## Business Recommendations
* **1. Enhance Customer Service**:
	+ Hire **15** additional support staff.
	+ Implement **AI-powered Chatbots** for 24/7 support.
	+ **Target**: Reduce complaint resolution time by **40%**.
* **2. Tailored Offers for High-Risk Segments**:
	+ Launch **"Young Achievers"** credit card with **2%** cashback on digital payments.
	+ **Target**: Engage **80%** of inactive credit card holders.
* **3. Streamline Digital Banking**:
	+ Invest in **$200,000** UX redesign.
	+ **Target**: Increase app ratings by **1 star** within 6 months.

## Next Steps
* **Implementation Timeline**: Q1 (Jan-Mar) for Customer Service enhancements, Q2 (Apr-Jun) for Tailored Offers, Q3 (Jul-Sep) for Digital Banking overhaul.
* **Budget Allocation**:
	+ Customer Service: **$500,000**
	+ Tailored Offers: **$300,000**
	+ Digital Banking: **$200,000**
* **Review and Adjustment**: Schedule a **6-month review** to assess strategy effectiveness and make data-driven adjustments.
