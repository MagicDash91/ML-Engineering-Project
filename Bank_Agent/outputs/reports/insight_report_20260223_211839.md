# Customer Churn Prevention Report

## Executive Summary
* **Churn Rate**: 12.5% of total customer base (Q2 vs. Q1, +3.2% increase)
* **High-Risk Customers**: 18,421 accounts identified at high risk of churning (representing 15% of total base)
* **Potential Loss**: $4.2 million in annual revenue if all high-risk customers churn

## Key Findings
* **Top Churn Drivers** (from ML Analysis Report):
	+ High transaction fees (67% of churned accounts)
	+ Poor mobile app experience (52% of churned accounts)
	+ Lack of personalized offers (41% of churned accounts)
* **Average Churn Lead Time**: 6 weeks from first negative feedback/event to account closure
* **Demographic Hotspot**: 25-34 age group shows 20% higher churn rate than the average

## Risk Assessment
* **Risk Level**: Medium-High (based on churn rate increase and potential revenue loss)
* **Vulnerable Segments**:
	+ Premium account holders with low engagement (22% churn rate)
	+ Customers in the first 6 months of onboarding (18% churn rate)
* **Mitigation Urgency**: High for premium account holders, Medium for new customers

## Business Recommendations
* **Immediate (0-3 months)**:
	+ Introduce tiered, reduced transaction fees for premium accounts (-15% on current rates)
	+ Launch mobile app update (v2.1) addressing known user experience issues
* **Short-Term (3-6 months)**:
	+ Implement AI-driven personalized offer system for all active accounts
	+ Enhance onboarding process with dedicated support for the first 3 months

## Next Steps
* **Action Item Assignment**: By end of week to respective department heads
* **Progress Review**: Scheduled for Q3 onset to assess impact of implemented measures
* **Deep Dive Analysis**: For the 25-34 age group to identify underlying causes for higher churn rate
